VR Escape Room

  • Watch Video

This program was created to educate statistics concepts permutation and combination in the form of an Escape Room type game. The game starts off within a jail cell in which the player is given a certain amount of time to figure out the three digit permutation to move on to the next room which will consist of colors where the player will have to guess the color combination to complete the game.

Wildfire VR

  • Watch Video

This program is created to help simulate a wildfire evacuation drill and demonstrate what would happen if a wildfire were to occur. The program starts off with the user inside a house where a phone will ring telling you to check the news After, the user will be instructed by an officer to follow the evactuation route and head to the safety point.
